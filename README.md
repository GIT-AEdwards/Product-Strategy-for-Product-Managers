# Product Strategy for Product Managers Nanodegree Program
A collaborative initiative by Accenture LearnVantage and Girls Who Code in partnership with Udacity. Was granted a scholorship out of many learners who applied.

# Scope of Program
The Product Strategy for Product Managers Nanodegree program is designed to equip aspiring product managers with the essential skills and knowledge needed to develop effective product strategies. Throughout the program, students learn how to identify market opportunities, define product visions, and create compelling business cases. The curriculum emphasizes practical applications, including market research, competitive analysis, and the development of key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success. By the end of the program, participants are prepared to make informed decisions that drive product development and align with business objectives.

# My scenario for my chosen project / product
"Hilton is developing a virtual concierge feature within its existing mobile app Hilton Honors, allowing guests to request room service, book spa treatments and activities, make dining reservations, and get personalized recommendations for local attractions. The goal is to make the guest experience seamless and increase revenue from concierge services."

# Purpose of Hilton Honors Virtual Concierge Project
The Hilton Honors Virtual Concierge project focuses on enhancing the guest experience within the Hilton Honors mobile app by introducing a virtual concierge feature. This feature allows guests to easily request room service, book spa treatments and activities, make dining reservations, and receive personalized recommendations for local attractions. The goal of the project is to streamline guest interactions, improve service efficiency, and ultimately increase revenue from concierge services, creating a seamless and enjoyable experience for Hilton guests.

# Project Objective (What I was asked to do)
As part of this project, I was tasked with researching the market opportunity for the virtual concierge feature, defining the target user personas, and creating a comprehensive business case. This involved calculating the Total Addressable Market (TAM), estimating development costs, and identifying key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the success of the feature. Additionally, I was required to develop a pitch deck that effectively communicates the value proposition of the virtual concierge, outlining its functionalities and how it aligns with Hilton's overall business strategy.
